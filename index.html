Elim Garak

Personnel file

Simple tailor

Elim Garak is a Cardassian tailor and Promenade shopkeeper of Garak's Clothiers who lives on Deep Space 9. He can do you a great costume for cheap. Whole Alpha Quadrant using his services.

What he wont do for you

  1. He wont murder your political opponent
  2. He wont spy on the government of your choice
  3. He wont fabricate data to force into war third party faction

What he will do for you

  • He will tailor you a good suit

Deep Space 9, Bajor system.
